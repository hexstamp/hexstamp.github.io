See your world fill in

A world map that grows with you.

Every country you visit shades in on your own world map. See how much of the world you’ve covered at a glance — continents, countries, and the percentage of the globe you’ve stamped.

Pinch, zoom, and tap a country to jump straight to your trips there.

Your passport, reimagined

A stamp for every trip.

Each trip earns a hexagon stamp in your passport — a honeycomb of everywhere you’ve been, coloured by continent. It’s the collection you actually want to keep growing.

Remember the trip, not just the place

Who you were with. What you did.

A country on a map is just the start. For every trip, keep the memories:

  • Tag the companions you travelled with — with a photo to remember their faces.
  • Add highlight photos from the trip.
  • Keep a dated journal of what happened, day by day.
  • Note the cities, regions and landmarks you saw.

At a glance

Your next trip, on your home screen.

Add the Hexstamp widget and count down to your next planned trip without opening the app — or see how much of the world you’ve seen so far. It refreshes each day.

Private by design.

Hexstamp has no server of its own. Your trips, photos and memories live only on your devices and in your own private iCloud — the developer never sees your data. No account to create, no tracking, no ads.
